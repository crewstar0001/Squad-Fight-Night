Situation

The ICO moved the goalposts

The Infantry Combat Overhaul is the most debated update in Squad's history. The argument was never really about realism — it was about whether combat should hinge on individual mechanical skill or on positioning and teamwork.

Offworld's stated goal was to lengthen firefights, reward coordinated squads, and reduce lone-wolf play by making aim less decisive. Plenty of players think that was the right call. Plenty of others miss the old ceiling — where a sharp shooter could carry a fight — and there just isn't an easy place for them to play it anymore.

That's the whole point of Fight Night. What the ICO changed:

  • Much heavier weapon sway
  • Stronger suppression effects
  • More stamina impact on aiming
  • Weapon stabilization + point / deadzone aiming
  • Picture-in-picture scopes
  • Bigger penalties for firing right after moving

Get on the server in 3 steps

1

Get the client

Fight Night runs on an older build, so you launch through a separate client — not your normal Steam copy. Download it and unzip it wherever you keep your games.
